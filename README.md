# Swiggy-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
I have built this comprehensive sales performance dashboard for Swiggy, which shows key business metrics that illustrate consumer behavior and present geographic growth opportunities. The dashboard will process more than 197K orders to derive actionable insights on revenue trends and service quality.
Peak Performance: The day when maximum revenue is generated is Saturday (₹7.78 Cr), marking a strong weekend peak in the food ordering industry.
Revenue Drivers: The contribution of Non-Veg food items into overall revenue (₹33.8 Cr) is higher compared to Vegetarian (₹19.2 Cr).
Top Market: Bengaluru is leading with sales of ₹5.46 Cr; followed by Lucknow and Hyderabad markets respectively.
